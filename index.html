History

Manger une pizza is a small family business that has been operating over three generations starting from 1960 to date with the aim of satisfying its customers with the best pizza.

We started the business from a typical door to door services to now having different branches all over the country serving more than 3000 happy customers a week.

Awards and events

As a long time serving institution, we have been able to pocket alot of awards voted by our loyal customers and in the process we have been able to host some of the greatest events.

Branches

Our branches are all over the country with our main branch in Nairobi westlands opposite Safaricom house.
